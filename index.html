About

...and what you can expect with us

Ethics

Top priority is given to ethical and transparent treatment. At all times, the patient is informed of treatment required, benefits, risks and cost involved. We believe in early detection and prevention of your dental problems to avoid complicated dental procedures at later stages.

Safety and Hygiene

Utmost importance is given to impeccable clinical hygiene and safety standards. We follow 4-stage sterilization technique in order to prevent any cross-contamination.

Technology

We use state of the art equipment and high class products for long term benefits. The clinic is equipped with latest digital X-Rays which substantially reduce radiation exposure and have higher accuracy. The doctors believe in continuously updating themselves with latest advances so that patients can benefit from newer techniques and products.

Cost-effective treatment

Specialist treatment is carried out for all dental problems at affordable prices. Patients can avail benefits of various packages and offers from time to time.

Friendly environment

We aim at eliminating the fear of dental treatment and providing a pleasant and comforting environment to our patients.
